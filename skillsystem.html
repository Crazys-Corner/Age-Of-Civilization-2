The Skill System

The skill system is a core design of our server. It ensures player trade and teamwork. As of Version 2.0, there are 3 skills:

- Blacksmith

- Miner

- Chef

Blacksmith

Blacksmiths are the only players who can craft iron, steel, diamond, and gold armor/weapons. They are also the only players who can craft guns!

Miner

Miners are the only players who can see ores. Ores appear as stone for other players.
